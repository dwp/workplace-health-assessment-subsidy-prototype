How the discount scheme works

How to use the discount code

  1. Check if your business is eligible and get a discount code.
  2. Search the list of providers.
  3. Contact a provider to book an assessment.
  4. Give the provider your discount code.
  5. After the assessment, you will get advice about free or funded onward support for your employee.
  6. Pay the provider when they invoice you.

If you need another discount code

You can use this scheme for all employees, including yourself.

Employees can have more assessments for ongoing or new cases.

For every assessment, you will need to get a new discount code by answering the eligibility questions again.

Discount codes do not expire.

What an assessment will cost

An assessment will cost between £20 and £80 after the 80% discount.

Why the cost of assessments differ

The price can be different depending on:

  • the complexity of the case
  • the type of health care professional that does the assessment

Examples

Sam has a video call assessment with a nurse. It cost £150, so his employer pays £30.

Emma has a an in-person assessment with an occupational therapist. It cost £250, so her employer pays £50.

If an employee needs to cancel or reschedule an assessment

Cancelling or rescheduling an assessment might result in a fee.

You need to check with the workplace health provider what their policy is.

If an employee does not attend an assessment

You will have to pay for the full cost of an assessment if your employee does not attend.

Reassuring and reminding your employee about the assessment process means they are more likely to attend.
