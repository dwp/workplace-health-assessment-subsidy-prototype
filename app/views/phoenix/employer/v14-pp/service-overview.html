Small and medium-sized enterprises can use this service to: 

  • get a code for an 80% discount on workplace health assessments for their employees
  • search for a private sector workplace health provider to do the assessment

After the discount an assessment will cost between £20 and £80. 

It will help you understand what support your employee needs to stay in or return to work.

Supporting an employee’s health and wellbeing may reduce the amount of time they need to take off work.

Find out more about how the discount scheme works.

Before you start

You will need to tell us:

  • where your company is registered
  • how many employees work for you 
  • what your yearly turnover is 
  • your contact details
  • your balance sheet total for your latest financial year

You do not need to provide any personal information about your employee at this stage.
