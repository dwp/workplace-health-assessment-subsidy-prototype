Using workplace health assessments to support an employee

Employees can ask you to refer them for a workplace health assessment.

You can also ask an employee if they'll agree to an assessment. This might happen if:

  • there's been a change in how they're performing at work
  • they've been absent more often than normal
  • they're off sick for more than 4 weeks  

Benefits to an employee

An assessment can help an employee:

  • return to work
  • get expert advice
  • get any support they might need  

If your employee does not agree to an assessment, remind them that they do not need to share information about their health with you.

Benefits to your business

Assessments help you understand how to support your employee’s health at work. This could help save you money in the future by:

  • reducing absences
  • increasing productivity
