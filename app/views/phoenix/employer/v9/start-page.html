Get an 80% discount on expert workplace health advice and recommendations for an employee

This service is primarily for employers. 

However, there is also advice for employees about how to get expert health support at work. .

Employers can use this service to:

  • get an 80% discount code to help pay for a workplace health assessment for an employee
  • search for a private sector workplace health provider to do the assessment

What a workplace health assessment provides

You should consider an assessment for an employee if they're finding it difficult to work because of their health. This could include:

  • their mental or physical health
  • an injury, such as a broken leg 
  • a long-term health condition
  • an impairment or physical disability  
  • a learning disability, for example ADHD or autism

This is a general list of health situations. If you’re unsure whether a specific health concern is a workplace health matter, you should discuss this when you contact a provider about doing a workplace health assessment.

A workplace health professional will not diagnose health conditions or disabilities. They will give advice and recommend onward support for an employee's health at work.

After the assessment, you and your employee will get a report with advice. This can help you understand what support they need to stay in or return to work. This could include:

  • advice on any reasonable adjustments you could make 
  • guidance on free and paid-for changes you could make to support them
  • links to other organisations who may be able to help 

See guidance on how a workplace health assessment works.

What you'll pay

Eligible companies get an 80% discount on a workplace health assessment. 

The cost of the assessment depends on which provider you choose and ranges from £100 to £400. After discount, the amount you pay will be between £20 and £80. 

Any further workplace health services are not discounted.

Who can use this service

You are eligible if your company is registered in England or Wales and you have one of the following:

  • 1 to 50 employees, and your yearly turnover is less than £10.2 million, or your yearly balance sheet is less than £5.1 million
  • 51 to 250 employees, and your yearly turnover is less than £22.8 million, or your yearly balance sheet is less than £11.4 million
  • 1 to 250 employees, and your company is less than 1 year old

What happens next

When you press start now, you will:

  • answer a maximum of 6 questions to find out if you're eligible
  • receive a discount code you can redeem with a provider
  • search for a workplace health provider to do an assessment

You do not need to provide any personal information about your employee at this stage.

If you get a discounted workplace health assessment through this service, we may contact you to gain feedback on the quality of the service you received.
