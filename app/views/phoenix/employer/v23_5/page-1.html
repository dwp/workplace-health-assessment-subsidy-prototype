What subsidised occupational health services can provide

Occupational health services can provide a range of support for employers.

They are usually paid for, but can save you time and money by helping to keep an employee in work.

Eligible businesses can buy certain occupational health services at an 80% discount. These are:

  • tailored advice about an employee's specific health condition or impairment
  • help with deciding on any changes you might need to make at work
  • workplace assessments
  • advice on supporting your employee to manage their health
