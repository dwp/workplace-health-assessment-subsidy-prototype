Find a workplace health provider

Use this part of the service to find a provider to give you an 80% discounted workplace health assessment for your employee.  

You can use filters to get a list of providers who most match your needs. 

Select a provider from the list, then contact them directly by email or phone. 

Most providers should be able to offer assessments for all conditions and situations, but you should check this when you contact them.

The provider will talk to you about:

  • the key details of your employee's situation
  • who they recommend to do the assessment - this could be a doctor, nurse or other health professional

They'll also discuss whether they need to see your employee in person. This could be for a range of reasons, such as: 

  • your employee has different ways of communicating
  • the provider may need to assess their workspace

What the assessment will cost

If you’re eligible for the 80% discount, the assessment will usually cost between £20 and £80.  

The actual cost of a workplace health assessment from our service is normally between £100 and £400.

Why the cost of an assessment varies
The cost of an assessment may vary due to factors such as:
  • the method of delivery. Most assessments will be by phone or video conference. However, some assessments may need an in-person or onsite meeting. These will usually involve an extra cost.
  • the complexity of the case. Naturally, some health issues are more straightforward than others.
  • who carries out the assessment. Some assessments can be done by a physiotherapist, while others may require a nurse or a doctor.

The final cost will usually be discussed and agreed upon during the initial conversation between the employer and the workplace health provider they have selected.

The 80% discount only applies up to an assessment cost of £400.

In some cases where an in-person assessment has been agreed, there may be additional travel costs. Any further costs over £400 are not discounted.

See examples of how the discount works

Example 1

The costs of each assessment in these examples are estimates. They are not meant to show what an individual health provider may charge for each assessment.

Telephone assessment by a physiotherapist.
Actual cost £100
Cost charged to employer after 80% discount = £20

Example 2

Video conference assessment by a doctor.
Actual cost £300
Cost charged to employer after 80% discount = £60

Example 3 (with travel costs)

In-person assessment by a nurse, at the employee’s home.
Actual cost = £200 + £100 travel costs = £300
Cost charged to employer after 80% discount = £60

In this case the total cost, including travel, does not go over the £400 limit. This means the entire cost qualifies for the discount.

Example 4 (with travel costs)

In-person assessment by a doctor, at the employee’s workplace.
Actual cost = £350 + £100 travel costs = £450
Cost charged to employer after 80% discount = £130

In this case the total cost, including travel, goes over the £400 limit. The 80% discount applies up to £400, and the remaining £50 cost above this is not discounted.
