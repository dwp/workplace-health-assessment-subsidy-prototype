Declaration

By sending the application you confirm that the information you have given to the Department of Work and Pensions (DWP) is complete and correct.

Using the service

You understand that:

  • you will need to pay 20% of the cost of any workplace health assessments you arrange
  • if the cost of the assessment is more than the DWP cap of £400 per assessment, for example, due to travel costs - you will need to agree in advance that you will pay the difference to the provider in full
  • you can only use discount codes with health care providers on the DWP register
  • If you need to make a complaint about a health care provider, you must contact them directly

Changes to the service

You understand that DWP may:

  • change the eligibility requirements
  • change the discount amount
  • pause or stop the service at any time

Liability

You understand that DWP is not liable for:

  • the quality of health care provider’s assessments
  • any losses or additional costs you may receive because of a health care provider

Your data

You understand that DWP:

  • will process your information as explained in the DWP Personal Information Charter (opens in a new tab)
  • will share your personal data with a research supplier contracted by DWP to independently evaluate the service
  • this research supplier may contact you to take part in voluntary research about the service you and your employee received
  • may do further checks on your organisation's eligibility

If you deliberately give wrong information or collude with a provider to exploit the service, you may:

  • be prosecuted
  • have to pay a financial penalty
  • have to pay back the full cost of any discounted assessments

DWP may update these declarations at any time.
