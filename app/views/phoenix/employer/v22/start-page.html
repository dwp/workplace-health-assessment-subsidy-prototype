Overview

Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) can use this service to get a code for an 80% discount of the cost of a workplace health assessment for employees.

An assessment will cost between £20 and £80, after the discount.

{#
You will be able to use this service to search for a private sector workplace health provider to carry out the assessment.
#}

The assesssment will help you understand what support your employee needs to stay in, or return to work.

Supporting an employee’s health and wellbeing may reduce the amount of time they need to take off work.

Pilot phase

During the pilot phase SMEs in Cumbria, Lancashire and Barrow-in-Furness have been invited to take part.

The pilot phase will be running from early 2024 for a number of months, after which we will open up the service to more employers.   If your organisation does not fit the eligibility for the pilot you can register your interest by emailing workplacehealth.employer@dwp.gov.uk.
