Payment process

Once you've carried out an assessment, you will need to invoice the employer and DWP.

Invoice the employer for 20% of the cost of the assessment. Invoice DWP for the remaining balance. You need to:

  • email the invoice to Shared Services Connected Ltd (SSCL) at APinvoices-DWP-U@gov.sscl.com
  • copy the email to DWP at: workplacehealth.provider@dwp.gov.uk

We aim to pay you 30 days after we receive your invoice.

DWP payments team will send you a purchase order number which you must use on every invoice you send to us.

You can invoice each assessment individually or put multiple assessments for different employers into one invoice.

What you must include in your invoice

Make sure it clearly displays the word ‘invoice’ and is addressed to:

DWP/SSCL invoices
PO Box 406
Newport
NP10 8FZ

If should also include:

  • the discount code
  • invoice number or unique identification number
  • the purchase order number we send you
  • your company name, address, and contact details
  • clear description of what you're charging for
  • the name of the employee's workplace
  • date the service was provided (supply date)
  • date the invoice was created
  • amount being charged (this could be one or more)
  • VAT amount (if applicable)
  • total amount owed

You also need to make sure your invoice:

  • has only the items that are on your purchase order
  • gives descriptions that match those on your purchase order
  • gives the supplier details that match those on your purchase order

If any of this information is missing, the invoice will be sent back to you.

Invoices need to be PDF files. Do no encrypt or password-protect them.

Sending your invoice to DWP

Send your invoice using email with your invoice attached as a PDF.

Your email should:

  • be no more than 4mb in size
  • give SSCL any supporting information in the invoices PDF file, for example, backing data

Make sure the subject line of your email says ‘Invoice’.

If the email and invoice does not meet these requirements, it will not be processed and will be sent back to you.

Support with invoices

If you need help with an invoice, you can call SSCL on 0845 241 5352. The phone line is open Monday to Friday, 9am to 4pm.

Or contact DWP at workplacehealth.provider@dwp.gov.uk if you haven't got the purchase order number.
