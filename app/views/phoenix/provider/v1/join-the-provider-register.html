Join the provider register

The register will list providers that have successfully joined the scheme. You can use this service to join the provider register.

What you will need

To join the register, we will need:

  • your full name
  • your job role at the company
  • the company name
  • the full name of your most senior healthcare professional
  • their GMC, HCPC or NMC number
  • a copy of their qualification
  • how you carry out your assessments
  • your bank details to add you to our payment system

After you're accepted onto the register

Once your details are published on the provider register, an employer can:

  • search the register to find providers
  • contact providers to discuss their employeeâ€™s needs
  • arrange an assessment

All their employees can get an assessment. Employees can have more assessments for ongoing or new cases.

For every assessment, an employer will need to get a new discount code.
