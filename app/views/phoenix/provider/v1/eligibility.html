Eligibility

You need to send us your most senior healthcare professionalâ€™s registration number and a copy of their qualification.

The senior healthcare professional must be a full-time or part-time employee. They cannot be a subcontractor.

This information will not be published on the register. It will be used to confirm that your organisation can provide quality assessments.

If this person leaves your organisation, you will need to give us the details of their replacement.

This service is for providers who can carry out mental and physical health assessments. If you are an occupational health physiotherapist or psychologist, you will be able to apply to the scheme later.

Evidence required

To join the provider register you will need to provide evidence of the qualifications of your occupational health professionals.

Occupational health physician

A GMC number and one of the following qualifications:

  • Diploma in Occupational Medicine (DOccMed)
  • Associates of the Faculty of Occupational Medicine (AFOM)
  • Members of the Faculty of Occupational Medicine (MFOM)
  • Fellowship of the Faculty of Occupational Medicine (FFOM)
  • any other required qualification that confirms your suitability as an occupational health physician

Occupational health nurse

A NMC number and one of the following qualifications:

  • Occupational health certificate, diploma, or degree recorded on Part 3 of the NMC register Specialist Community Public Health Nursing (SCPHN)
  • any other required qualification that confirms your suitability as an occupational health nurse
  • Occupational therapist with experience in workplace settings, vocational rehabilitation, or return to work

A HCPC number and one of the following qualifications:

  • Degree (BSc or MSc) in Occupational Therapy
  • any other required qualification that confirms your suitability as an occupational health therapist
